## Two different clocks

The slogans are almost all about the **70-year clock**.
Your next decade — the **10-year clock** — is a different list.

| 10-year clock (you, now) | 70-year clock (lifetime) |
|---|---|
| Don't start nicotine | Smoking |
| Alcohol & acute injury | Physical inactivity |
| Road (belt, helmet) | Alcohol |
| Gambling on a phone | Diet / food environment |
| Sleep & mood | Gambling · Sun · Sleep |

## Smoking kills — stopping helps

- Smokers die on average **~10 years** younger — British Doctors Study (Doll et al., BMJ 2004), reproduced in two other countries.
- Two-thirds of lifelong smokers are killed by it (Banks et al. 2015).
- **But**: stopping before 40 avoids **>90%** of the excess risk; before 30, **>97%** (Pirie et al., Lancet 2013).

A threat **plus an exit**. Threat alone produces fatalism.

## Gambling: designed so you lose

No statistics needed. The edge is a **published design parameter**.

- Roulette house edge: **2.7%** (single-zero), **5.26%** (double).
- UK online slots return-to-player: **92–96%** — specified to keep 4–8%.

> You are not unlucky. You are on the wrong side of a number
> the company **chose in advance, wrote down, and published.**

<!--

online slots vs 8.9% sports betting), not the personality — the tar-delivery

## The number that was never a finding

**"10,000 steps a day"** is a 1960s pedometer brand name.

- *Manpo-kei* = "10,000-step meter", sold by Yamasa around the **1964** Tokyo Olympics.
- The character 万 (10,000) even looks like a walking figure.
- Actual data: mortality plateaus at ~**6,000–8,000 steps/day** in over-60s, **8,000–10,000** in under-60s (Paluch et al., Lancet Public Health 2022).

The friendliest proof that a number can circulate for 60 years without ever being evidence.

<!--

the biggest gain on the curve is just NOT being in the least-active group
(Ekelund et al. 2019). No round number to hit.

## The slogans that failed

<div class="worked">

| Worked (describes the world) | Failed (describes *you*) |
|---|---|
| Smoking kills | Just say no (DARE) |
| Back to sleep | Drink responsibly |
| Slip, slop, slap | When the fun stops, stop |
| Clunk click, every trip | 10,000 steps |

</div>

The left column is public health. Much of the right was written by the **seller**.

## You cannot out-willpower a design.
### But you can see it.

*Smoking kills — stopping helps. Alcohol: no safe dose. Gambling: designed so you lose.*
*Move more, sit less. Sleep like it matters. Slip, slop, slap.*

**And when someone tells you to enjoy their product "responsibly" — ask who benefits from that sentence.**
